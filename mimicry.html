Mimicry by Piyush

My name is Piyush Talegaonkar. Being a film enthusiast, I am learning to be a part time filmmaker. The mimicry is an attempt proceeding in the process. The performance is a mimicry of Dr. Prashant Umape Sir and Pankaj Patil Sir. Here, Patil sir's beautiful handwriting provokes him to use it for more artistic purposes and to challenge his thought is the more scientifically driven Umape sir. What follows is a hilarious back and forth between the both of them, in an attempt for both of them to justify themselves. In the end I would like to express to all the professors that we love them very much and you have formed a lot of beautiful parts of our memory.
